◆ IDP VERDICT · GREEN BAY 2026
Three seasons of historical data confirm Franklin's IDP value was entirely built on his Indianapolis role: every-down deployment, 60+ snaps per game, and 90% box rate. His 2024 ceiling season (10.5 fpts/gm, 11 TFLs, 5 FFs) and consistent 2023–2025 miss rates show no talent regression. The move to Green Bay is a role regression, not a talent regression.

In Green Bay he is the 4th linebacker in a crowded room. Cooper plays every down. McDuffie is expected to take the MIKE role Walker vacated. Hopper is the established 3rd option. Franklin enters on a prove-it deal with zero guaranteed dead cap — the front office already has an exit ramp. At 15–30 snaps per game, the tackle volume that generated 9.9–10.5 fpts/gm over two full seasons at Indianapolis simply does not exist.

The only IDP scenario where Franklin has value in 2026: Cooper or McDuffie misses multiple games. If that happens, monitor immediately — his historical snap volume and production rates show he can step into an every-down role and deliver LB2 output within 1–2 weeks of full deployment. But price him as a handcuff, not a starter. Do not pay for Indianapolis production numbers in Green Bay fantasy drafts.
